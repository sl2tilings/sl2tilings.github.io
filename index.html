This website is devoted to the project on using a combinatorial approach to number theory and geometry that arise around Conway–Coxeter friezes and SL2-tilings over integer numbers and other rings, carried out at the Open Univeristy, UK.

The project is funded by EPSRC grants EP/W002817/1 and EP/W524098/1.
